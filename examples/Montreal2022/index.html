Introduction

Modeling Galaxy Orientations using Graph Neural Networks

Overview/Motivation

Weak Lensing

  • This bending of light tells us a lot about the matter content and the energy content of the Universe
  • We measure the coherent shape distortions of m(b)illions of galaxies
  • However, few challenges. One important nuance: galaxies are not randomly oriented on the sky

Overview/Motivation

Intrinsic Alignment (IA)

  • Intrinsic Alignment (IA) is the tendency of galaxies to align with their neighboring galaxies and the underlying large scale structure.
  • This effect can masquerade as a weak gravitational lensing signal
  • Contributes to the systematic errors of weak lensing surveys.



Image credit: Troxel & Ishak

    Overview of Deep Generative Models

    • Diffusion models are the current state-of-the-art on image, molecule, etc ...
    • Diffusion models need extensions to non-Euclidean spaces

    Overview of (Denoising) Diffusion Models

    • The data is gradually corrupted by adding noise, until it becomes almost complete noise
    • The goal is to learn the reverse process, so we can generate new samples from the latent space
    • Also, very closely connected are the Score-matching (Score-based) generative models
